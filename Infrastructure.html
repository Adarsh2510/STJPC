School Infrastructure

Classroom

School has spacious classroom each, well equipped with light, furniture, fans and related teaching aids ergonomically designed to suit children's need for space, light and facilities are tailored to their size and capabilities, Digital Classrooms are also available with projector and speaker facility to make children keep in touch with modern technology"


Science laboratories

We have well-equipped laboratories for imparting practical knowledge in Physics, Chemistry and Biology for class IX to XII,All the laboratories are well-stocked with sufficient chemicals, specimens and instruments so that every student gets the opportunity to carry out the experiments individually. Well-qualified and experienced teachers guide the students to explore the world of science through a meticulously planned schedule of practical classes. Moreover, the safety of every child while handling the chemicals and instruments is duly taken care off.


Computer Laboratories

In An Attempt To Equip.The Students With The Modern Stream Of Information Technology.School Has Modern Computer Lab.It Has Multimedia; Internet etc.Computer Education Has Been Introduced From Class III To XII.Monthly Edition Of Various Computer Magazines Keep One Abreast With Modern Advantages.The computers in the labs have been upgraded with appropriate software to enable students of classes XI and XII to execute various program as per the syllabus

Library

The School Has Well Stocked Library Covering All The Subjects,Subscribes All Important Newspaper(Both In English & Hindi) And Available Magazines In English & Hindi & Periodicals.The Students Are Encouraged To Develope The Habit Of Reading With the aim of inculcating a lifelong passion for books and knowledge, we encourage all students to make the most of their library sessions, They Can Borrow Books For Home As Per Library Rules varying from classes to classes.

Sports Facility and Extra Curricular

The school firmly believes in the maxim ’’A healthy mind resides in a healthy body”. In order to promote all-round development of students, a complete range of sports and games facilities with all sorts of cultural activities are provided by school to students for which the school has a lovely garden and a playground. The garden is specially designed to keep the children into a healthy environment.

Transport Facility

The School has adequate number of buses available for students covering almost all nearby localities. Here Parents Are Requested To Transport Their Ward Where The Stoppage Has Been Made By The School For The School Bus for any query refer to contacts section.
